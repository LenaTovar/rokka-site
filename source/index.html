rokka (ロッカー) is the Japanese word for locker.

rokka makes storing your images easy and neat, just as a locker does the same for your items in the real world. You can hand all the responsibilities of storage and serving different sizes to rokka, using a simple REST API to do so.

Why choose rokka?

Made for the web

We built rokka on today's web best practices. It only delivers over https for example, and sets headers to maximize caching on clients.

Learn more

The cleaner the leaner

rokka will not confuse you with thousands of features which you pay for at other services but then only use a small part of it. We concentrate on a few features of high cleanliness. And we promise to stay lean.

Learn more

Simple API

The rokka API is as simple as it can be. On top, we offer libraries for common languages, making rokka even easier to use.

Learn more

Upload once, show often

It can't be easier: upload your image in the highest possible resolution. Then configure your formats for how images should be output. Done. rokka takes care of the rest.

A dash of SEO for everyone

With rokka you can have the exact words you want in the image name. You can change it too, without any API calls. Simple and fast.

Auto cropping

Besides simple resizing, rokka can also handle complex auto cropping operations with user definable subject areas. You can create optimized versions for all your needs. We make sure to continually improve the toolbox for image manipulation.

Extensive documentation

Everything in rokka is documented. Besides a detailed technical reference, multiple guides and examples will have you up and running within minutes. More time to spend on developing than integrating.

Lightning fast delivery

No matter where in the world your viewers are, they are getting the image delivered in the fastest way possible, every single time. This also saves your server resources.

We're just getting started.

We are still in beta and are looking to find a couple interested projects that want to work closely with us to make sure rokka does everything you need - obviously for free. Don't fear the after-beta. We stay fair to our early clients and testers. You only pay what you use. No fixed fees, nothing hidden. We will charge monthly for storage and a usage fee for bandwidth of delivered images, that's it.
